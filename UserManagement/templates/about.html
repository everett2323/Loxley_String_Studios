About

Chris Loxley is a music teacher, performer, and father located in the Westchester town of Pelham, just north of NYC. He has spent the last 2 decades back in NYC after earning a Master's Degree in Strings Performance from CU Boulder, to accompany his existing Music Education & Performance Major from Ithaca College in undergrad.


Chris keeps up with performances with indie bands such as 'Shrines' (https://www.thisisshrines.com/), his wedding duo Penguin Music (Weddings tab), but also can be found in his home studio educating school students of Colonial, Hutch, Siwanoy, PMS and PMHS.
